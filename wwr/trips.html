Explore Our Rafting Adventures

Choose from thrilling whitewater experiences or scenic river journeys.

Family Float

This two hour family float is the perfect adventure for small kids. With a minimum age of 4, and plenty of opportunity to splash in the waters of the Nile, this trip provides a wonderful morning adventure for families.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Skill Level: Beginner
  • Includes: Guide, safety gear, refreshments

Wild Canyon Run

Challenge yourself on our most thrilling whitewater course through the narrow canyon. This 5-hour adventure offers Class IV rapids, steep drops, and unmatched adrenaline.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Fitness Level: 8 years Minimum weight 30kg.
  • Includes: Lunch, guide, all safety gear

Grade Five White Water Rafting

Relax and enjoy a gentle float perfect for families or anyone seeking calm waters and wildlife views. Includes fun activities for kids and a riverside picnic.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Skill Level: 16 years minimum.
  • Includes: Snacks, guide, family games
